06 / 09
Quality checks

Two passes catch what you'd miss.

Every piece of content runs through both checks before you see a single word. The AI patterns get stripped out automatically.

Check 01
Slippery slope
Every sentence pulls the reader into the next. No isolated paragraphs. Bridge sentences between every idea shift.
Check 02
Negative parallelisms
"No X. No Y. No Z." gets caught and rewritten into natural sentences every time.
Check 03
AI vocabulary
"Game-changer", "seamless", "unlock", "revolutionary" and 9 other AI words get stripped on sight.
Check 04
Hedge language
"Maybe", "might", "potentially", "I think" all get removed. Your content states facts, not opinions.
Check 05
Rhythm variation
Same-length sentences feel algorithmic. The pass varies sentence length so it reads like a human wrote it.
Check 06
+ 6 more rules
Em dash overuse, rule of three, sycophantic tone, fragment openers, duplicate subjects, bolded headers.

07 / 09
Built-in framework

Sultanic copywriting principles come loaded.

Proven mechanics from Alen Sultanic baked into every piece of content the skill creates. You don't need to learn them. The skill applies them for you.

01
4-line block rule
Never more than 4 lines per paragraph. The eye scans for density before the brain reads for meaning.
02
"Install" tweak
Replace "learn" or "discover" with "install." It implies zero effort and instant utility.
03
"In 2026" tweak
Add the current year to headlines to double conversions. Answers the question: does this still work?
04
"Different" tweak
In saturated markets, don't promise better. Promise different. Reframes the entire category.
05
Away to Towards
Start with pain. Shift to aspiration in the final 20-30%. The arc that makes people buy and stay.
